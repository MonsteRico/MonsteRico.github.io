Levels need to be .pngs made in paint or similar program.
Size of a level file is 64x32 pixels.

Dark Brown, Dark Brown, Dark Brown  (Antigrav Down, Antigrav Down, Antigrav Down) - Decreases gravity to 3rd level

Light Brown, Light Brown, Black  (Antigrav Up, Antigrav Up, Wall) - Increases gravity to 2nd level
Light Brown, Light Brown, Light Brown  (Antigrav Up, Antigrav Up, Antigrav Up) - Increases gravity to 3rd level
Bright Blue, Black, Black  (Ice, Wall, Wall) - Increases friction to 1st level
Bright Blue, Bright Blue, Black  (Ice, Ice, Wall) - Increases friction to 2nd level
Bright Blue, Bright Blue, Bright Blue  (Ice, Ice, Ice) - Increases friction to 3rd level
